[03] Standard Terms
Apply to all engagements unless varied in writing.
Payment
50% deposit to confirm booking; balance on delivery. Retainers billed monthly in advance.
Revisions
2 rounds of changes included. Further rounds billed at the editor day rate.
Rush
+25% surcharge on briefs requiring delivery in under 5 business days.
Equipment
Hire shown as estimate, reconciled at cost +10% handling. Final package confirmed at recce.
Expenses
Third-party costs (talent, travel, equipment hire, licensed music, stock) passed through at cost +10%.
Usage
Quoted fees cover owned/organic channels — website, EDM, Instagram, Pinterest, YouTube. Paid media, OOH, and broadcast quoted separately.
Currency / GST
All figures AUD. Exclusive of GST — confirm GST treatment before invoicing.
